  • Screw It

    • Design + Code

    How many Psychiatrists does it take to change a lightbulb? Or programmers? Or surrealists? We don't know, but with this self-centrered repairman it only take one to hold the bulb - the world rotates around on it's own! Well, not quite - some stubborn household items are in the way. Repair the light by act of creative re-decoration, make one succesfull rotation of the whole room and... SCREW IT!
